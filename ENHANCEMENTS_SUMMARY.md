## â“ Common Questions

**Q: How do I know when my order is ready?**  
A: Check the order timeline. When status changes to "Served", your food is ready at your table.

**Q: Can I print the bill later?**  
A: Yes! E-bills are available anytime for completed orders. Just go to Order History and click the order.

**Q: What if I need to see order details from last week?**  
A: All orders are saved in Order History. Scroll down to find older orders and click to view details.

**Q: How do I collect payment for cash orders?**  
A: After marking order as "Served", click "Payment Received" button when customer pays. This completes the order.

**Q: Can multiple staff see the same orders?**  
A: Yes! All staff logged into the restaurant account see the same orders in real-time.

**Q: What if customer asks about their order?**  
A: Click the order card to see the complete timeline. You can tell them exactly when each stage happened.
